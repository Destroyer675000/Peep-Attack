<Peep Attack by Corey Flickinger>

Genre

Shooter

Story

One day, a scientist by the name of Neil deGrasse Tyson wanted peeps. But Neil deGrasse Tyson did not want any old peep.No, he wanted to create the best peeps around. So Neil deGrasse Tyson went hard to work in figuring the best combanaiton of flavors and ingredients to make the best tasting peeps in the universe. Even though he is a great scientist, he is a bad cook and as a result, he created peeps that were alive. At first they seemed innoncent but that is when they started to attck. Your mission is to destroy this cute creation.

Esthetics

Graphics style - will be taken off the internet and will be simple in theme

Sound - I am thinking of taking sound bites that are related to peeps

Gameplay

Mechanics

The player will move need to avoid the oncoming waves of peeps by either firing their heat ray or by dodging the peeps. The main goal is to last as long as possible with 3 lives and also to score the highest amount of points.

Control

  • Touch- Will be used to fire the gun to attack oncoming peeps.
  • Accelerometer - will be used to move the player around the screen.
